Our Mission and Purpose

Exotic-Bay is the longest established exotic animal and reptile shop in Glasgow, and one of only a handful that have traded for three decades in the UK. It has a reputation that can only be earned over a long period of time! 

Exotic-Bay was made by four enthusiasts like yourself and we love to share our knowledge and expertise! But beware, our enthusiasm is infectious! We can help you, no matter what your level within the hobby.

Exotic-Bay offers the largest selection of exotic animals in the UK with unusual and infrequently seen for sale species our specialty. At any one time,we have over 200 reptiles, amphibians, invertebrates and other exotic animals for sale.

Exotic-Bay is also proud to be the only pet selling online store with their very own delivery service. This enables us to deliver to in anywhere in the United Kingdom!

Exotic-Bay Team!

At the heart of Exotic-Bay are the founders Ans Farooq, Daniyal Khan, Tom Anderson and Stephen Graham. It is well known in the hobby not only for Exotic-Bay Exotics but also for his committee work with the various well known Societies and Federations within the UK, including the IHS which he first joined in 1980. By the early 1990’s, we was one of the largest snake breeders in the UK, specialising in Colubrids and small Boid species. We opened Exotic-Bay in 1993, the longest established exotic animal specialist in the heart of Glasgow and one of a handful in the UK that have traded for such a long time. We regularly contribute to magazines, journals, TV and Radio programmes – and even movies. We also hold a City and Guilds in Herpetology!
